# The Wingman Project: 10 Concept Brands

## 1. VANTABLACK
**Tagline:** "Visibility is overrated."  
**Niche:** Tech/Gaming peripherals  
**Product Line:** Matte black desk accessories, cable management, minimalist monitor stands  
**Vibe:** Stealth luxury. No logos, no RGB. Pure function wrapped in obsidian.  
**Color Palette:** #0A0A0A (True Black), #1C1C1C (Charcoal), #808080 (Gunmetal)  
**Typography:** Helvetica Neue Ultra Light, all caps, letter-spaced  
**Target Influencer:** Tech reviewers, minimal desk setup YouTubers (Marques Brownlee aesthetic)

## Brand Application Strategy

Each brand will have:
1. **Logo:** Wordmark + icon (generated via AI, refined in Figma)
2. **Hero Product Shot:** Lifestyle photography style (AI-generated, studio-quality)
3. **Landing Page:** Single-page site with quiz funnel, social proof, Shopify checkout
4. **Packaging Mockup:** Unboxing-ready presentation

These 10 brands showcase our range:
- **Aesthetic diversity:** From cyberpunk (KINETIC) to wabi-sabi (SOLACE)
- **Niche coverage:** Tech, wellness, fitness, travel, home, beauty, fashion, food, pets, productivity
- **Conversion focus:** Each site will have a unique quiz/engagement hook
